About

Daily Money is a simple & easy financial application. It is also an open source project. you could find the project/source here : Daily Money (YES, It is OPEN and FREE to use. I will re-public the new source when I feel it is good to show)

Features
  • Record your daily expense, income, asset and liability.
  • Count balance of money.
  • Export/Import to CSV.
  • Export Database to SD and restore when new installation.
  • Balance pie chart & time chart.
  • Password protection.
